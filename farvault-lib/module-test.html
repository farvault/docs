(async, static) fundRegtest(mnemonic, fundingDescriptors) → {Promise.<object>}

Source:
  • test/tools.js, line 45

Creates an HD wallet and funds it using mined coins from a regtest-server faucet. It then mines 6 blocks to confirm payments.
